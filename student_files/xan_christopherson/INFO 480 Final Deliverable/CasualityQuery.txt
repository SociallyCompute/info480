After importing the raw casualty data and the two character country codes, this query yields the desired dataset, Minus the occupation code

SELECT [SERVICE]
      ,[DEATH DATE]
      ,[AGE]
      ,[OCCUPATION CODE]
      ,[CASUALTY CITY]
      ,[CASUALTY STATE]
      ,CASE WHEN tl.[CASUALTY COUNTRY] = cc.AD THEN cc.Andorra 
			WHEN  tl.[CASUALTY COUNTRY] = 'IZ' THEN 'Iraq' ELSE 
					tl.[CASUALTY COUNTRY] END AS CasualtyCounty
      ,[CASUALTY CATEGORY]
      ,[ID]
  FROM [finance].[Ops].[testlocations] tl
  
  LEFT JOIN finance.ops.countrycodes cc
  ON tl.[CASUALTY COUNTRY] = cc.AD
